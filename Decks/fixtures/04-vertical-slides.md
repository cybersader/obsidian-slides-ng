# Vertical slide nesting

`---` separates horizontal slides. `--` (two dashes) makes the next
slide a vertical sub-slide of the previous one. Press Down to descend
into a stack.

---

# Stack A — entry

This is the top of a vertical stack.

--

## A.1 — sub-slide

Press Down from the entry slide to get here.

--

## A.2 — sub-slide

Another vertical step.

---

# Back to horizontal

This is the next horizontal slide (after the A stack).

---

# Stack B — entry

Another stack.

--

## B.1

Sub-slide of B.

---

# Final horizontal slide

End.
